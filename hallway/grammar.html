🎯 Mission: Classifiers (量詞)
Classifiers come before the noun,
When you count, let's break it down!

Use 個 (go) for people:
One person is 一個人 (jat1 go3 jan4).

Use 隻 (zek) for animals:
One dog is 一隻狗 (jat1 zek3 gau2).

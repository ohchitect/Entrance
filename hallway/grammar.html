Ordering Tim Hortons Coffee (Location Prepositions)

今日，溫哥華天氣好凍。
好多人喺 Tim Hortons 入面買咖啡。
我喺櫃檯前面等。
Gam1 jat6, Wan1 go1 waa4 tin1 hei3 hou2 dung3.
Hou2 do1 jan4 hai2 Tim Hortons jap6 min6 maai5 gaa3 fe1.
Ngo5 hai2 gwai6 toi2 cin4 min6 dang2.

📚 Grammar & Vocabulary Prep

Prepositions (介詞): Expressing where things are.

  • 喺 (hai2): At / In / On
  • 入面 (jap6 min6): Inside
  • 前面 (cin4 min6): In front of

10 Key Vocabulary Words:

1. 天氣 (tin1 hei3) - Weather
2. 凍 (dung3) - Cold
3. 買 (maai5) - To buy
4. 咖啡 (gaa3 fe1) - Coffee
5. 櫃檯 (gwai6 toi2) - Counter
6. 等 (dang2) - To wait
7. 好多人 (hou2 do1 jan4) - Many people
8. 今日 (gam1 jat6) - Today
9. 入面 (jap6 min6) - Inside
10. 前面 (cin4 min6) - In front of

📝 Knowledge Check

1. 點解好多人買咖啡？ (Why are many people buying coffee?)
2. 我喺邊度等？ (Where am I waiting?)
3. "Inside" 點講？ (How to say "inside"?)
4. "In front of" 點講？ (How to say "in front of"?)
English Story Translation:
Today, Vancouver's weather is very cold.
Many people are buying coffee inside (入面) Tim Hortons.
I am waiting in front of (前面) the counter.

❓ Help - Cannot Hear Cantonese

📱 Mobile Device (iPhone/iPad)

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to Accessibility
  3. Tap Speech
  4. Tap Languages or Voices
  5. Search for "Chinese (Hong Kong)" or "粤語"
  6. Tap the download button (⬇️) next to the voice
  7. Wait for the voice to install

🤖 Android Device

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to Accessibility
  3. Tap Text-to-speech options
  4. Select your preferred text-to-speech engine
  5. Tap Install voice data
  6. Choose "中文 (香港)" or "粤語"
  7. Download and install the voice

💻 Windows/Mac Computer

Most desktop browsers have limited voice support. Try:

  • Using Chrome or Firefox browsers
  • Make sure your system has Cantonese voice installed in system settings
  • Check your Operating System's text-to-speech settings

⚠️ Still Not Working?

If you've installed the Cantonese voice but still don't hear it:

  • Try refreshing the page
  • Close and reopen your browser
  • Check that the voice is actually installed on your device
  • Make sure your device's volume is not muted
